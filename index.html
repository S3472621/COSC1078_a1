Project Idea

Overview

The project I envision is an online platform by the name of shisha base. It revolves around the idea of a crowdsourced shisha review and recommendation platform based on machine learning. The front end will be user facing, which will allow individuals to rate shisha flavours and shisha products based on personal experience. A database is then integrated into the back end which collates data input from the online community while separating different products based on constituent ingredients, product code, brand, potency, and other variables. Machine learning coded into the backend will then recommend future products to members based on the products they have rated.

Motivation

A motivating factor for this project was my personal experience in searching for a favourable shisha flavour outside the mainstream products. For several years my options were limited to only that which was tried and tested, my trusted apple-mint by the brand Al-Fakher. Whilst occasionally an endeavour was initiated to try other flavours, a repeated lack of success in finding a solution led me gave up on my quest. This was mainly due to lack of access to information available online as site recommendations often turned out to be completely contrary to what reviews stated. This is mainly because flavour websites often recommend products based on commercial viability rather than a customer’s tailored taste.

This all changed upon the discovery of a reddit thread where a group of like-minded individuals presented me with their recommendations. Niche flavours which surprising turned out to be the highlight of my shisha experience thus far. This was the time a decision was made for the creation of the virtual platform shisha base. A haven for likeminded shisha aficionados to explore and experiment with options they never knew existed.

Description

The project at hand would require the coding of a front-end user-based review system to build a database of flavours, constituent ingredients, brand, and aggregate rating. Customers will create a profile and begin a journey of discovery. They will select their set-up by choosing from existing products such as shisha bases, heads, accessories, and flavours.

When a user’s product is not available in our database, they have the option of adding said product which is then reviewed and accepted into our database for future users.

The rating system will be implemented to identify favourability of not only retail products but also the constituent ingredients. This system will be a 0-10 based system, where 10 is highly favourable and 0 very unfavourable.

Machine learning is used to determine likelihood of a user liking a product based on ratings of other products as well as commonality amongst their peers.

Example 1;

User A likes flavours: Love66, Angel Lips, Punk Man and Blue orange.

User B likes flavours: Love66, Punk Man, Blue Orange and 3 Angels

Shisha Base proceeds to recommend 3 Angels to User A and Angel Lips to User B.

Also possible is the mixing of certain products to create new and potentially favourable flavours.

Example 2;

Multiple users rate Flavours A & B highly. Correlation between users liking Flavour A if they like flavour B and vice-versa is found.

Flavour A: Love66 (passion fruit, honeydew melon, tropical passion fruit, mint)

Flavour B: Lady Killer: (melon, mango, and berry)

Shisha Base will recommend the option of mixing Flavours A + B to create new flavour.

A weekly automated data analysis in the back end will be published which shows most liked flavours weekly and newly discovered mixes with highest performing attributes published on the front page.

A tailored recommendation list will also be published for each individual user on their home page. The rankings of the recommendations are to be based on their ratings + other individuals rating of products based on similar likes and dislikes.

A social aspect of the project will include a section where users can discuss products, set-ups, and other relevant products. This will produce a collaborative space for users to discuss experiences and ideas in an open forum where flow of information will enable others to learn from each other freely and intuitively.

A specialised section where manufacturers and users can interact will also allow feedback to be more easily accessible and enhance the flow of information from both sides.
